DIY Manicure Guide

Step-by-step instructions for perfect nails at home

1

Remove Old Polish

Start with a clean slate by removing any existing nail polish. Use an acetone-free remover for a gentler approach, especially if you have dry or brittle nails.

  • Soak a cotton pad with nail polish remover
  • Press onto the nail for 5-10 seconds
  • Wipe away in one direction
  • Repeat until all polish is removed
2

Shape Your Nails

Use a nail file to create your desired nail shape. Always file in one direction to prevent splitting and damage to the nail.

  • Choose your shape: square, round, oval, or almond
  • File from the outer edge toward the center
  • Use gentle, smooth strokes
  • Keep both hands symmetrical
3

Soak and Soften

Prepare your cuticles by soaking your fingertips in warm water. This softens the skin and makes cuticle care easier and more comfortable.

  • Fill a bowl with warm water
  • Add a few drops of cuticle oil or soap
  • Soak nails for 3-5 minutes
  • Pat dry with a soft towel
4

Push Back Cuticles

Gently push back your cuticles using a cuticle pusher or orange stick. Never cut your cuticles as this can lead to infection.

  • Apply cuticle remover or oil
  • Use a cuticle pusher at a 45-degree angle
  • Gently push back in small circular motions
  • Clean under the nail tip
5

Buff and Smooth

Use a buffer to smooth the nail surface and remove any ridges. This creates a better base for polish application.

  • Use the coarse side to smooth ridges
  • Buff in one direction only
  • Use the fine side for shine
  • Don't over-buff - 2-3 strokes per nail
6

Clean and Prep

Remove any dust and oils from the nail surface. This ensures better polish adhesion and longer-lasting results.

  • Wash hands with soap
  • Wipe nails with alcohol or nail prep solution
  • Let nails dry completely
  • Avoid touching nail surface after cleaning
7

Apply Base Coat

A base coat protects your natural nails from staining and helps polish adhere better. Never skip this step!

  • Apply one thin, even coat
  • Start from the center, then sides
  • Cap the free edge of the nail
  • Let dry for 1-2 minutes
8

Apply Color

Apply your chosen nail polish color in thin, even coats. Thin coats dry faster and last longer than thick ones.

  • Wipe excess polish off the brush
  • Apply using 3 strokes: center, left, right
  • Let first coat dry before second
  • Usually 2 coats are sufficient
9

Apply Top Coat

Seal your manicure with a top coat for shine and protection. This extends the life of your manicure significantly.

  • Wait for color to dry completely
  • Apply one even coat
  • Cap the free edge
  • Reapply every 2-3 days for longevity
10

Clean Up and Moisturize

Perfect your manicure by cleaning up any mistakes and nourishing your cuticles and hands.

  • Dip small brush in remover for cleanup
  • Apply cuticle oil around each nail
  • Massage in hand cream
  • Avoid water for at least 1 hour

Pro Tips for Long-Lasting Manicures

  • Always shake your polish before use to mix the formula evenly
  • Keep your polishes stored upright in a cool, dark place
  • Wear gloves when doing dishes or cleaning
  • Apply cuticle oil daily to keep nails healthy
  • Touch up chips immediately to prevent further peeling
  • Let each layer dry completely before applying the next
  • Don't use your nails as tools!
